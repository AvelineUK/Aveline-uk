Privacy Policy

Effective date: #

This Privacy Policy explains how I handle information on this website.

Information Collected

The only personal information collected is what you choose to provide when you use the contact form or send me an email. This may include your name, email address, and the contents of your message.

How Your Information Is Used

Your information will only be used to respond to your inquiry or communication. It will not be sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Data Retention

Messages sent via the contact form or email may be kept for record-keeping purposes, but will not be used for marketing without your consent.

Third-Party Services

This site does not use tracking cookies, analytics, or advertising tools that collect personal information.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to keep your information secure, but no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your Rights

If you would like your information deleted, please contact me directly and I will remove it from my records.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, you can reach me at: email@example.com
