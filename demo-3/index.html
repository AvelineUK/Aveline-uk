How we can help

Our services are designed to address every aspect of your financial life, from building wealth to protecting what matters most.

Investment Management

Bespoke portfolio construction and ongoing management aligned with your risk tolerance and objectives. We employ a disciplined, research-driven approach that looks beyond short-term market noise.

Retirement Planning

Comprehensive pension reviews, contribution strategies, and drawdown planning to help you achieve the retirement lifestyle you envision. We navigate complex regulations to optimize your outcomes.

Tax Planning

Proactive strategies to minimize your tax burden while remaining fully compliant. From capital gains planning to inheritance tax mitigation, we help you keep more of what you've earned.

Estate Planning

Thoughtful structuring of your estate to ensure your wealth passes to future generations efficiently and according to your wishes. We coordinate with legal professionals to implement robust solutions.

Protection Planning

Safeguarding you and your family against unforeseen circumstances through appropriate life assurance, critical illness cover, and income protection policies.

Business Owner Advisory

Specialized guidance for entrepreneurs and business owners, including exit planning, shareholder protection, and remuneration strategies that balance personal and business needs.

Our investment philosophy

We believe successful investing requires discipline, patience, and a long-term perspective. Rather than chasing performance or timing markets, we focus on asset allocation, diversification, and cost control.

Every portfolio we construct is unique, reflecting your individual circumstances, time horizon, and capacity for risk. We regularly review and rebalance to ensure your investments remain aligned with your goals.

Fee structure

We operate on a transparent, fee-based model. Our charges are clearly explained before you become a client, with no hidden costs or commission-driven conflicts of interest.

This alignment of interests ensures we're focused on what matters: helping you achieve your financial objectives. Many clients find that our advice more than pays for itself through improved outcomes and peace of mind.

About Meridian Wealth

Founded in 2003, Meridian Wealth has grown from a single-office practice to one of the region's most respected independent financial advisory firms.

Our story

Meridian Wealth was established with a clear vision: to provide financial advice that truly serves clients' best interests. Our founders, having worked within large financial institutions, recognized the conflicts inherent in commission-based models and set out to create something different.

From day one, we committed to independence, transparency, and putting clients first. Today, we manage over £1.8 billion on behalf of more than 950 client families across the UK.

Our team

Our advisors hold advanced qualifications including Chartered Financial Planner status and are committed to ongoing professional development. Many have specialized expertise in areas such as pension transfers, inheritance tax planning, or investment management.

Behind every client relationship is a dedicated support team ensuring smooth administration and exceptional service. We believe expertise matters, which is why we invest heavily in our people.
