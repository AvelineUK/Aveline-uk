FAQ

Your questions answered

I've put together a list of some questions you might have.

If you can't find the answer here, please don't hesitate to contact me.

General

How does it work?

Firstly, contact me via the contact page to begin the initial consultation phase. Tell me as much as you can about what you'd like for your site, and I'll get back to you as soon as possible.

  • IMPORTANT: the more detail you can give me at this stage, the smoother the process will be. Changes later can cost extra.

When I have a clear understanding of your needs, I'll send you a contract to sign. Once that contract has been signed, I'll begin work on your website, and I'll keep you updated on the progress. This is when you'd send me any content you want on the website, such as images and text.

When the site has been built, I'll send you a link to take a look. At this stage, I'm happy to make non-structural changes, such as colours, effects, images, etc. However, if you'd like me to make any structural changes, such as new pages or changing the page layout, that will incur an additional fee. This is why it's so important to be as detailed as possible during the initial consultation phase, as anything agreed upon during that phase will be included in the ongoing monthly fee.

After any changes have been made, the site will go live soon thereafter. If you have a domain you'd like me to connect to the website, I can do that for you too.

And... that's it! You'll be able to make unlimited content changes (such as images or text) to the site going forward simply by emailing me. I'll make sure to monitor the site regularly, to ensure everything is running smoothly. If everything goes according to plan, you should never have to worry about your website ever again.

And how does the monthly plan work?

You pay a single monthly fee, and that's it! No hidden or extra costs. Unless you decide to add pages or change the structure of the website, the monthly fee is all you'll pay.

Is there really no upfront fee?

That's correct! With the exception of some optional add-ons, which are not included in the standard packages, there is no upfront fee. Once you sign the contract, the monthly fee is all you need to pay.

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?

No. All contracts are month-to-month. When you pay, you're paying for the following month, so if you decide to stop at any point, you can without any cancellation penalties. All I ask is that you give me thirty days notice before you intend to cancel.

Please note that if you cancel without giving notice and decide to re-activate your website again in the future, you will be required to pay for the months between your initial cancellation and your request for re-activation before the site will be re-activated.

What happens if I cancel?

The website will remain online until the end of your billing period, at which point it'll be taken offline.

Who actually owns the website?

Unless you decide to pay the Fixed Buyout Fee, which will be specified in your contract, I retain ownership of the site. However, the ownership of the domain name, as well as any images or content you provide to me will, of course, remain yours.

Can I buy the website outright later?

Absolutely! In your contract, there will be a Fixed Buyout Fee specified. If you decide to pay that fee at any time, you will become the sole owner of the entire website. If you decide to pay that fee and want me to continue to host and manage your site, I can do that too. But that would be up to you!

Pricing & Billing

When are payments taken each month?

Payments are taken each month on or around the date your first payment was made. So, if you signed up on 23 November, then all subsequent payments will be taken on or around the 23rd of each month going forward.

Do prices ever change?

With the exception of the annual price adjustment, which I explain in the following question on this page, your monthly fee will not increase.

How does the annual price adjustment work?

Each year in April, I may increase the monthly rate, either by £5, or the UK Consumer Price Index (CPI) plus 3%, whichever is greater. If the CPI method is used, I will use the figure from the preceding December.

What happens if my payment fails?

If a payment fails, the payment system will automatically attempt to make the payment three times: two, five, and seven days later. If the payment fails after the third attempt, the site will be taken offline and I will treat it as a cancellation without notice.

If you think you're not going to be able to pay, please let me know. The site will still be taken offline, but you won't need to make any extra payments once you're able to pay to have it back online, whereas you would if you didn't notify me.

What happens if I stop paying?

Your website will be taken offline.

If you give me notice beforehand, then you won't be required to make any payments if you'd like to re-activate the site again in the future.

If you stop payment without notifying me and wish to re-activate the site again in the future, then you'll be required to pay for the months the site was offline before the site will be re-activated.

Is VAT included in the price?

At present, I'm not registered for VAT, so VAT is not charged on any of my prices. Should I become VAT-registered in the future, I'll inform all clients in advance, and any changes to pricing will be communicated clearly before taking effect.

What is your refund policy?

I provide ongoing website design, hosting, and maintenance services on a subscription basis. Because these services begin immediately and involve continuous time, hosting, and resources, all payments are non-refundable once collected. This also means that the statutory 14-day cooling-off period under the Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013 does not apply once the contract has been signed.

Of course, if there's ever an issue or you're unhappy with my work, please reach out. I'll always do my best to make it right.

Design & Content

What's included in my monthly plan?

Below is a summary of what's included in the plan:

  • Website build: I make your website with no upfront fees.
  • Ongoing hosting: Your website is reliably hosted.
  • Security monitoring: Your site will receive all the necessary security protection.
  • Unlimited content edits: You can update the content on your site as much as you'd like.
  • Unlimited form submissions: Customers will always be able to contact you.
  • Zero headaches: Once the site is made, you'll never have to worry about it ever again.

What's the difference between the 1-page and 5-page plans?

One is a single page, the other can have up to five pages. That's the only difference. Everything else is the same between the two plans.

Can I have more than 5 pages?

Of course! However, this will come with a higher monthly fee. Please see our pricing page for more details.

How do content updates work?

If you want to change any of the content on the website, then just e-mail me what you'd like to change, I'll make the changes as soon as possible, and I'll let you know when the change is live. It's that simple.

What counts as a “larger change”?

Adding extra pages, changing page layouts, or any other change that involves changing the code or underlying architecture of the website. If you're not sure if a change you'd like to make would be considered a larger change, then feel free to contact me and we can discuss it further.

How long will it take for you to build the website?

This will depend on a number of circumstances, including how complex the project is, how much detail you provide, and how quickly you can provide me the images and content you'd like on the site. However, as a general rule of thumb, most sites take a few weeks to make, test, and deploy.

Can you redesign my site later?

Absolutely! This would be considered a larger change, and would incur a separate cost. But if you want a new design for your site, that is absolutely something I can do for you.

Do you write the text for me?

No. Copywriting is something I do not offer. I always encourage clients to write their own content because they know their business better than I ever could.

Can you use my existing logo and photos?

Of course! Just send them to me, and I'll use them on the website. I may compress the images so that they load faster, but the image itself will look identical to the one you send.

Hosting & Technical

Where is my website hosted?

I host all of the websites I make on Netlify.

Will my site be secure (SSL, backups, etc.)?

Absolutely. Your site will always have an SSL certificate, and the site will be securely stored in multiple locations, ensuring that nothing will ever be lost.

Can I use my own domain name?

You can! If you're comfortable to do it, then you can arrange that at your end. However, I always recommend that the process of sorting out the domain be something that I manage solely from my end, as it's usually simpler. If you're comfortable allowing me to manage your domain, then I'll be happy to do so.

Do I need to buy hosting separately?

Not at all! Hosting is included in the monthly plan.

Can I move the website elsewhere?

If you'd like to, then you absolutely can. However, you would need to pay the Fixed Buyout Clause specified in your contract before doing so.

Do you provide email accounts?

I can set up an email account for your site, but this service is not included in the monthly plan and would be an additional charge.

Will I lose my website if I stop paying?

If you stop paying, the site will be taken offline. But I keep every website I make, ensuring it can be restored later on if you wish to do so.

Support & Maintenance

What kind of updates are really “unlimited”?

In short: content changes. That includes text and images. Making changes to either of these is genuinely unlimited.

How quickly do you make changes after I request them?

As soon as I can! I never like to make promises as to how quickly I can make and deploy new changes, but content change requests are always my main priority, and I will let you know when the change has been made.

What happens if my site goes offline?

If the site went offline due to non-payment, then it simply remains offline until the situation has been resolved.

However, if the site has gone offline unexpectedly, then you can be assured that I will do everything to ensure that service is restored as soon as possible. However, this is extremely unlikely to happen. Netlify, the service I use to host all of my client's websites, is extremely reliable, with an almost 100% guaranteed uptime.

Do you back up my website?

Absolutely, in multiple locations. While I never make promises, the likelihood of losing your website data is as close to zero as it can possibly be.

Can you add new features later (forms, galleries, etc.)?

I can, but this would likely fall into the "larger change" category, and would therefore incur an additional charge.

Service areas
